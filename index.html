Inspired by her excellent culinary skills, Gabina's Cuisine continues to excel in providing great traditional Filipino food that offers culinary delight appreciated by all those who have tasted it.

Our Menu
Chicken Adobo Plate Combo

A flavorful combo featuring tender chicken adobo, three crispy lumpia, and a satisfying mix of steamed rice and savory pancit.

BBQ Combo Plate

Your choice of juicy chicken or pork BBQ skewers, served with steamed rice, pancit, and a side of crispy lumpia.

Pancit Plate

A generous serving of savory stir-fried noodles with vegetables, perfect on its own or as a hearty side.

Vegetable Lumpia

Crispy golden spring rolls filled with a savory mix of seasoned vegetables, served with a flavorful dipping sauce.

5 Piece Lumpia

Deep fried savory snack made of thin crepe pastry skin called "lumpia wrapper" enveloping a mixture of chopped vegetables (carrots, cabbages & celery) with minced pork.

Fried Chicken Skin

Addictively crispy, deep-fried chicken skin, seasoned to perfection for a crunchy, savory snack.

Filipino Turon

A crispy, caramelized banana spring roll, filled with jackfruit and fried to golden perfection.

Visit us at your local Farmers' Markets

Experience agriculture and enjoy great Filipino food while you buy local San Diego produce when visiting one of the Certified Farmers’ Markets (CFM) in San Diego County.

Farmers’ Markets provide venues for farmers to sell directly to consumers and support small farming operations and small local businesses as well.
